Shortbread cookies




description

These three-ingredient shortbread cookies melt in your mouth. The secret is in the beating.

ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 and 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white suger

    steps to cook

  • Step 1

    • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C.)

  • Step 2

    • Combine butter or margarine, flour, and confectioners' sugar; beat for 10 minutes.

  • Step 3

    • Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto an ungreased cookie sheet.

  • Step 4

    • Bake in the preheated oven for 15 to 18 minutes or until bottoms are lightly browned.
